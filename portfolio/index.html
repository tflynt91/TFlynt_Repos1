About me

I am a percussionist with years of professional experience. In the orchestral setting I have played under a number of world renowned conductors and musicians.

In the chamber music setting I have played a wide variety of solo and small chamber pieces with different ensemble arrangements. I have arranged many piano pieces for my vibraphone and marimba duo.
